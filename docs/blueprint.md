- Homepage Welcome: A calm and reassuring introduction to Nabarrocoaching, emphasizing safety and trust over sales.
- Instructor Profile: An 'About Nathalie' section building trust by showcasing experience and mission, focusing on human connection and guidance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep water blue (#2962FF) to convey trust and safety.
- Secondary color: Light pool blue (#B2EBF2) for a sense of calm and openness.
- Accent color: Warm soft yellow (#FFF9C4) to highlight key reassurance messages and titles, promoting confidence and warmth. 
- Font pairing: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text providing human readability with warmth, paired with 'Playfair' (serif) for headlines to lend elegance to headings.
- Simple, friendly icons supporting content without distraction, tailored to adults. Icons communicate water safety, calm movement, and balance.
- Slow visual rhythm with plenty of whitespace, clear headings, no pop-ups, or forced funnels, ensuring a calm and accessible browsing experience.
- Subtle animations to support a slow and calm visual rhythm.
